Project Idea

The project is on developing a 3D rhythm game that is a first-person shooter which should be played with a mouse and keyboard to click to the target, sort of like an aim trainer while shooting targets to the beat.This project should have a target audience of gamers who enjoy playing shooters. It will however only be useful for people who aim to improve at their favourite first-person shooting games. The image below shows the video game sales in the United States in 2018. 20.9% of video games that are bought are classified as shooters, meaning most gamers favour this genre over most genres, as portrayed in the image. Furthermore, I feel that a lot of people who enjoy this type of genre will also enjoy my project idea.

As it is a 3D rhythm first-person shooter game made primarily to help people to improve their aim in shooters, there will be targets, which look like enemy terrorists to shoot with the gun they are holding in the game with the focus of improving their flicking and reaction time. If you do not shoot it in time, it will shoot you deducting 10 of your health from 100. The targets will appear one at a time at different positions, and disappear when you shoot it, recurring until the end of the time limit. Scores are exponential and a headshot will reward the most points, followed by the body and finally the legs, so if they miss the target, their combo is broken. The targets will also appear along with the beat of a music simultaneously, hence adding a rhythm ‘feeling’ to the game and making it a rhythm first-person shooter game. Cocos2d is a free software for developing games using the program languages such as Python, C++, C# and Objective-C. I would want my game to be written in C# language only because osu!, a 2D circle clicking rhythm game was written in C# as well. The skills that are required to do this project is to simply know how to write codes. If this project is successful, gamers who mostly like the shooting genre can learn mechanics such as flicking and reaction time and can improve their aim in other games they enjoy, while clicking the targets to the beat of the music.
